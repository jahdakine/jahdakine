2009-2011. Complete tracking system involving grant applications from inception through five years of progress reporting and potential monetary recapture. Multi-user access control included applicants, reviewers (both technical and process), process owners, stakeholders, auditors, reort consumers, and super-users to access different areas of the application. The process involved deadlines for review, award, and recapture; automatic review assignment and email reminders; and voluminous reporting - including jobs created data forwarded directly to the administration. This is by far the most complex application I have developed to date, resulting in a President's Award from NREL.

  • Technologies
    • Database: SQL Server
    • Scripting: ColdFusion
    • Documentation: DokuWiki
    • Bug Reporting: Trac
    • Version Control: Subversion
