User Agreement

If you click "I agree to the terms of the user-agreement," in essence it means absolutely nothing. This is only a demonstration. Had this been a real user agreement you would have fallen asleep at the terminal while the cat finished your lukewarm coffee and the z, x, c, and v keys became indelibly stamped onto your forehead. And, upon awakening, you would have scrolled down to the bottom (because you know we check to see if you did that) and closed the document without reading or understanding a thing.
