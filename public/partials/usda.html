2003-2007. The Energy Title of the 2002 Farm Bill included Section 9006, which directed the Secretary of Agriculture to create a program to help farmers, ranchers and rural small businesses pay for renewable energy and energy efficiency projects (Union of Concerned Scientists). Read more... NREL sponsored the program's online technical review and funding recommendation management system. The site enabled applications processing and review along with customizable ad-hoc reporting. Each year, new applications were received through a new portal that separated them by date. Reporting could be aggregated across all year sets or filtered.
